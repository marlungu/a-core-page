About

Designing data, AI, and system architecture with clarity and intent.

What Archetype Core is

A technical body of work for engineers who want systems that stay reliable as they scale. The focus is not tools or trends, but the architectural decisions underneath them.

  • Education-first: frameworks, diagrams, and patterns grounded in principles.
  • Systems mindset: reproducibility over ad-hoc fixes and one-off scripts.
  • Applied integrity: examples use anonymized or synthetic data when needed.

Focus

Where the work concentrates:

  • Data platforms and warehouse architecture
  • ETL/ELT orchestration and reliability
  • Applied AI, agents, and workflow design
  • Governance, identity, and observability

How to use this site

Start with Notes for the most concrete work. Use Learn as the map of how the thinking is organized. Join the Blueprint if you want new material delivered consistently.
